Ines de Ramon holds two Colored Stone Essentials certifications from the Gemological Institute of America, according to her Linkedin profile.

Ines De Ramon's Childhood In Switzerland

Ines de Ramon is a private person, with private social media accounts, so little is known about the designer. According to New York Post, the 32-year-old grew up near Lake Geneva, between France and Switzerland, in a posh neighborhood in Cologne. Her father reportedly worked in finance and her family originally hailed from Madrid, Spain.

The Swiss press reported that Ines' high school friends said that she was “among the most popular girls at the school.” The article also reports that she was an avid runner at the local middle school. While her native tongue is French, de Ines is proficient in German, Spanish, Italian, and English.

In 2014, Ines often appeared in local socialite magazines like Cote, appearing in party shots with prominent architects. She then moved to New York before recently setting in Los Angeles for work.
