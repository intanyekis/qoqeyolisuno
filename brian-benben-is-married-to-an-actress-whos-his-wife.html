Brian Benben is Married to an Actress; Who's His Wife?

August 2024 · 4 minute read

Brian Benben‘s outstanding portrayal of Martin Tupper in the HBO TV series Dream On earned him the fame he has today. The American actor with a courteous character has credited in 27 movies to date.

Besides Dream On, he has also appeared in a medical drama series Private Practice. To know more about the star’s personal life, stick with the article.

Married to an Actress

The star is in a marital relationship with Madeleine Stowe. Madeleine is also a talented actress like Brian himself.

The duo first crossed path while filming for the show The Gangster Chronicles. They co-starred as husband and wife in the short-lived TV show.

Ultimately, they found a connection in-between and started dating. After dating for some time they realized they were ready to spend the rest of their lives together in real life as well and tied the knot on 8 August 1982.

The Benbens share a beautiful daughter, May Theodora Benben who was born in 1996. Theodora is a Psychology graduate of Duke University. It seems the couple prefers a rather small family and is happy with only a single kid.

Robbed of $75,000 Worth of Jewelry

In May of 2016, news arose that the couple was robbed at gunpoint at their own home. The duo was soundly asleep when they were awoken by ruckus coming from their bathroom.

Naked, Stowe went towards the sound to discover an unknown man. The wife was eager to understand what was happening when the unknown figure pointed a gun towards her. The robber then demanded money to which she denied at first but later had to submit to the gun carried by the man.

The 61-year-old handed over an antique jewelry collection that was reportedly worth over $75,000. After acquiring the jewelry, the robber made a run for it. The frightened couple, wasting no time, reported the police right after the incident but the culprit was nowhere to be seen.

After a series of investigations, the police arrested the suspect Walter Canizalez for the breach. Turns out, the suspect was also involved in 15 similar home invasion cases. The court later granted Stowe a protective order against Walter.

Benben’s Seven Figure Net Worth

Brian Benben owns a net worth of whopping $8 million. The actor is best recognized for his performance in the HBO television series Dream On as Martin Tupper.

The man also starred in the TV series Private Practice for the role of Dr. Sheldon Wallace in 2008. He also produced a sitcom, The Brian Benben Show on September 21, 1998, which didn’t last very long, ending after only a month of airing.

Owner of a Ranch

The partners in crime live in a two-story house made out of limestone located in Texas. The house stands on a 400-acre cattle ranch which they own.

Brian’s wife came across the ranch while taking a drive, so as to take a break from her stuffy shooting schedule. She stumbled across the landmark while driving northeast from Brackettville toward the Hill Country. She instantly fell in love with the location and penned her name into the ownership papers in 1994.

The ranch is home to several lively animals as antelopes, horses, swans, and sheep. Stowe named her favorite horse Sherman. They insist the family take care of the ranch in “the Cowboy way.”
