George Lee Biography

On July 29, 1953, Mary Weinrib and Morris Weinrib welcomed Gary Lee Weinrib into the world in Ontario, Canada.

He was brought up as a Jew and had his bar mitzvah at the age of 13. Geddy Lee went to Fisherville Junior High School in Willowdale, Toronto, Canada. He started performing at the age of nine.

While still in high school, Lee and his friends turned their basement into a practice area. To pursue a profession as a performer, he left school.

Career

Together with Neil Peart and Alex Lifeson, Geddy Lee co-founded the rock group Rush in 1968. A year later, they started doing professional performances at social gatherings, high school dances, and coffee shops. They started writing and performing original music in taverns and clubs. The group started giving live performances in 1972.

In 1974, Rush released their first studio album under their name. A year later, they put out two albums, “Fly by Night” and “Caress of Steel.” The movie “2112” came out in 1976. In September 1977, the album “A Farewell to Kings” was released. Hemispheres then came after that.

Their 1980s albums included “Permanent Waves,” “Moving Pictures,” “Signals,” “Grace Under Pressure,” “Power Windows,” “Hold Your Fire,” and “Presto.” In 1991, Rush issued the album Roll the Bones. The movie “Counterparts” came out in October 1993. They released their album Test for Echo in 1996.

In 2002, Rush issued “Vapor Trails.” Snakes & Arrows, which debuted in 2007, came after this. Their most recent studio album, “Clockwork Angels,” was released in 2012. In addition, they have released 78 singles, 32 music videos, 10 video albums, 11 live albums, six box sets, one EP, and nine compilation albums.

With the release of his solo album “My Favorite Headache” in 2000, Geddy Lee made his solo debut. He took on the role of producer for the Boys Brigade CD in 1982.

Geddy Lee Awards and Achievements

He was made an Officer of the Order of Canada in 1996. He was also admitted to the Juno Hall of Fame in 1994. Along with his band Rush, he has also been awarded several additional titles and awards.
