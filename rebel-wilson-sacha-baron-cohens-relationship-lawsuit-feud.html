No one could blame Rebel Wilson for keeping her baby private after she realized that the public would scrutinize every little thing that she did. In this article, we'll take a look at what kind of relationship Wilson and Baron Cohen had before this incident. We'll also look at why he allegedly said he would pursue legal action.

What Was Rebel Wilson And Sacha Baron Cohen's Relationship Before Their Feud And Alleged Lawsuit

According to Variety, Rebel Wilson said that Sacha Baron Cohen harassed her on the set of the 2016 movie The Brothers Grimsby. While Sacha Baron Cohen's Borat was a profitable and star-making vehicle, some of Baron Cohen's other movies failed at the box office, and Grimsby didn't do very well. In an interview on the radio back in 2014, she said he harassed her on the final shooting day. Based on her comments, it seems that they had a terrible, negative, and unprofessional relationship.

It's unclear if the actors met before shooting the movie. Since they haven't said they did, it seems their time together on set was their only interaction.

Wilson said that Baron Cohen spoke inappropriately to her and kept talking about body parts. He allegedly said:

“Look, I’ll just pull down my pants, you just stick your finger up my butt, it’ll be a really funny bit.”

The actress wasn't comfortable with that, and she also didn't like that he kept telling her to take off her clothes during a scene. She said:

“Every day he’s like, ‘Just go naked, it will be funny. Remember in Borat when I did that naked scene? It was hilarious. On the last day, I thought I’d obviously won the argument and he got a body double to do the naked scene.”

It sounds like Wilson has spent several years thinking about this disturbing interaction.

In 2017, Cafe Mom reported that people speculated that Baron Cohen sexually harassed Wilson. The actress tweeted, "even I have a story to tell." Fans figured out that she shared the same story in her 2014 radio interview and in her 2017 tweets.

Why Did Sacha Baron Cohen Allegedly Threaten To Sue Rebel Wilson?

According to Variety, Sacha Baron Cohen allegedly wanted to sue Rebel Wilson for writing about his on-set behavior in her memoir, Rebel Rising. On March 15, 2024, she wrote on Instagram:

“When I first came to Hollywood, people were like, yeah, ‘I have a no a**hole policy, means like, yeah, I don’t work with a**holes.’ I was like, ‘Oh yeah. I mean, that sounds sensible or logical. But then it really sunk in because I worked with a massive a**hole and yeah, now I definitely have a no a**holes policy.”

On March 25th, 2024, Wilson explained that Sacha Baron Cohen was the person she was referring to, and she wasn't afraid to say more.
