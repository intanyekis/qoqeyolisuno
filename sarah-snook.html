Who is Sarah Snook? 

Sarah Snook is a 34-year-old Australian actress. She is well known for her roles as Jane and John in Ethan Hawke’s Predestination, as well as Gertrude “Trudy” Pratt in The Dressmaker. Sarah has received numerous prizes and acclaim for her work in these two films. In 2019, she starred in the critically acclaimed HBO series “Succession.” She portrayed Siobhan “Shiv” Roy in the film. This turned out to be her most memorable role to date.

She gained widespread critical acclaim and was nominated for her first Primetime Emmy for outstanding supporting actress in 2020. Sarah was confirmed as the replacement for American actress Elizabeth Moss in the lead role of the upcoming film Run Rabbit Run on December 6, 2021. She just returned to her native Australia, where she will be presenting at the next Australian Academy Cinema Television Awards, also known as the AACTA Awards, on December 8th, 2021.

Sarah Snook’s Professional Life

Sarah made her acting debut in the 2011 live-action film Sleeping Beauty, playing a minor role. She had her first successful main part in the Australian romantic film Not Suitable for Children in 2012. In which she starred as Stevie. She received her first Film Critics Circle of Australia Award and was nominated for an AACTA for her role. Sarah made her television debut in 2009 as Sophie, a character in the hospital drama All Saints. She went on to appear in several additional Australian-based shows. She earned the AACTA award for best actress in 2014 for her part in Predestination.

Sarah was cast as Andrea “Andy” Cunningham in the Oscar-winning movie about Steve Jobs, The Last of Us, in 2015. In 2016, she appeared alongside Anthony Mackey in the hit Netflix show Black Mirror. In 2018, she received a lot of attention for her part in HBO’s comedic series Succession. Sarah Snook was cast as the classic literary heroine Anne Eliot in Jane Austen’s version of Persuasion in September 2020. Sarah will officially replace Elizabeth Moss in Daina Reid’s next film Run Rabbit Run in December 2021.
