What is Kate Gosselin’s net worth and salary?

Kate Gosselin, an American reality television personality, is worth $500,000. She is best known for her role in the reality series Jon and Kate Plus 8. The show, which aired from 2007 to 2009, featured Gosselin as a mother of eight children, including a set of sextuplets and a set of twins. Unfortunately, the show was put on hold following Gosselin’s divorce from her husband, Jon.

Kate Gosselin’s Background

Kate Gosselin was born in Philadelphia on March 28, 1975, to Charlene and Kenton Kreider. Her father worked as a pastor, and she was one of five children in the family. After completing her nursing diploma program at a medical center in Pennsylvania, Kate worked as a labor and delivery nurse. It was during this time that she met her now ex-husband.

Birth of Gosselin Twins and Sextuplets

Kate met Jon Gosselin at work in 1997, and they got married less than two years later. Kate had to undergo fertility treatments due to her polycystic ovarian condition, and she gave birth to twin girls, Cara and Madelyn, in 2000. Kate underwent more fertility treatments and gave birth to sextuplets – Aaden, Collin, Joel, Alexis, Hannah, and Leah – in 2003. The sextuplets were born 10 weeks premature, and in May 2020, they turned 16 years old.

Kate Gosselin’s Reality TV Career

Kate Gosselin, her husband Jon, and their eight children were featured in the NBC show Home Delivery, which chronicled the renovation of their Pennsylvania home. The family was also featured on Discovery Health in Surviving Sextuplets and Twins in 2005, and in Sextuplets and Twins: One Year Later in 2006. They were then signed to do their own reality show, Jon & Kate Plus 8, which debuted in April 2007. The show started on Discovery Health before moving to TLC in later seasons.

Kate and Jon Gosselin’s Custody Battle

In 2009, the reality TV couple Jon and Kate Gosselin finalized their divorce, with Kate being awarded the family home and primary custody of their eight children. In August 2016, Kate announced that their son Collin, who reportedly has special needs, had been placed in an inpatient program. A year later, police were called when Jon and Kate got into an argument over their daughter Hannah. In April 2018, Jon was granted custody of Hannah and in December 2018, he was granted custody of Collin as well. The two ex-spouses now only communicate through their lawyers and Collin and Hannah are estranged from their siblings that live with Kate.

Kate Gosselin’s Career Highlights

After her divorce, Kate and her children starred in a series of specials called Kate Plus 8. The show was canceled during season five. She also competed on Dancing With The Stars in 2010 and appeared on Celebrity Apprentice with Donald Trump.

In 2013, Gosselin and her kids were featured on an episode of Celebrity Wife Swap, where she switched places with former Playboy Playmate Kendra Wilkinson. Gosselin also published four books: Multiple Blessings in 2008, Eight Little Faces in 2009, I Just Want You to Know in 2010, and Love Is in the Mix in 2014.

In 2019, TLC debuted Kate Plus Date, where Gosselin enlisted the help of expert matchmakers in her journey to find love. However, the show only had one season.

Kate Gosselin’s Earnings

Kate Gosselin earned $250,000 per episode on her show Kate Plus 8. She and her ex-husband Jon earned between $25k-$40k for speaking engagements and church tours, which included cash from church collection plates and charging fans for photos and autographs. She also made $500k from her appearances on Dancing with the Stars.

Kate Gosselin’s Home and Property

Kate Gosselin still resides in the Pennsylvania home that was featured on her hit reality show, Jon & Kate Plus 8. The five-bedroom home was purchased by Kate and her ex-husband, Jon Gosselin, over 10 years ago and sits on a 24-acre property. The 6,000 square foot home is valued at $1.1 million.

Kate currently lives with six of her eight children in the home, with only Cara and Mady being away at college.
