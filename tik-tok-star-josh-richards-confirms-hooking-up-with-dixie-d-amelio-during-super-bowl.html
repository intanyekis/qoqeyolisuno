Dixie and Josh were frequently seen together on multiple occasions, particularly fueling romance rumors when they attended the Super Bowl alongside Charli and Landon Barker, which seemingly appeared to be a double date.

Josh, however, debunked these rumors in an interview, saying he and Dixie were “just friends.”

But months later, it seems he’s changed his tune, as told in a recent episode of the 'BFFs' podcast.

“This is before you two were kind of even messing around," Bri asked.

Portnoy then said, “Alright so, headline: Josh and Bri confirm that Josh was hooking up with Dixie back around the Super Bowl,” Josh then said, "Okay."

When Dixie D'Amelio talked about starting podcast with Josh Richards

Earlier, in an episode of the 'D'Amelio Show', Dixie addressed her relationship with Josh Richards, while talking about their anticipated podcast.

Dixie said, "I'm working on starting a podcast with Josh Richards, and it is a relationship advice podcast, which is awesome because we're both single and had very public breakups."

The TikToker and singer added, "It's just, like, really smart for it to come from us."

"He asked me to do it in November, December, and that's when we started talking more, and then we started hanging out as friends, and then … I just hate that there was so much drama around him and me," Dixie shared.

She concluded, "Whatever. It'll be fine. It'll be fun."
