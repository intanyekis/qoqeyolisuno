Joyce Hawkins, mother of American singer-songwriter Chris Brown showed off her makeup-free face in a selfie, and fans could not get over the look.

In a new post on her Instagram feed, Joyce Hawkins, mother of American singer, dancer, and actor, Chris Brown, showed off her bare face in a makeup-free selfie, and fans could not stop gushing.

In the photo, Hawkins was seen standing by a clear blue swimming pool as she posed. She had her curly black and red hair packed into a messy bun with the locks cascading down the sides of her face.

Hawkins' face was also partially hidden behind a matching red and black face mask. She was clad in a red attire that matched her whole look, but the rest of the view was hidden outside the picture frame.

She also had no makeup on and the sun did a great number on her smooth skin as it shone through the trees overhead. Some fans praised her beauty while others expressed their love for her hair. Beneath the picture, she wrote:

"ALL MASK IN ALL STYLES SOLD OUT!!! THANK YOU SO SO MUCH. I STILL HAVE A FEW OF THE BUNNY MASK LEFT IN KID SIZE ONLY!!!"

Hawkins is not new to dazzling her fans with beautiful pictures of her age-defying look. Over the last months, her youthful look has continued to remain a topic of discussion among her followers.

Last month, she posted a stunning selfie on her Instagram feed that left her fans gushing. In the photo, she had her dark luscious curls packed up into her signature bun with the rest of the locks cascading down the sides of her face as she gazed into the camera.

Hawkins had on hot but simple makeup that completely accentuated her features. She also wore large dangling earrings and a low necked dress. However, what struck her fans the most was how young she looked in the selfie.

Unsurprisingly, fans soon speculated that she had gotten fillers while others concluded that she went under the knife for cosmetic surgery. Whether or not the speculations are correct, Hawkins continues to remain at the top of her game.

The grandmother of two has successfully joined the ranks of celebrity mothers who have still got it going. On the list are actress Taraji Henson, socialite Kris Jenner, and many others.

Besides being a glam Hollywood mama, Hawkins never fails to look out for her son. Over the years, Brown has been at the center of several controversies and drama due to his erratic behavior.

In an interview in 2011, Hawkins opened up to discuss his past and her support for him. She revealed that despite his grave mistakes, he was still a human being and he needed to own them and move on with his life.
