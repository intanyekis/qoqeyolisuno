Jessica Simpsons Friends Reportedly Worry How Shell React If Her New Music Flops

July 2024 · 3 minute read

Highlights

  • Jessica Simpson plans to relaunch her music career after a decade-long hiatus.
  • Friends and family are concerned about her ability to handle a failed comeback.
  • Simpson's past struggles with addiction and alcohol raise concerns about her mental health and potential relapse.

Jessica Simpson is apparently looking to relaunch her music career, despite years of rumors that her health is suffering. But a new report says that those close to Jessica are concerned about what will happen if her career comeback fails, as they fear she won’t handle it well.

“Jess feels she needs to do this and dreams of topping the charts again,” a source told Life & Style. “She just wanted to wait until her kids were old enough to understand that their mother is an entertainer and she may be away from home performing.”

Jessica’s Friends Allegedly Think Her New Music Will Flop

While Jessica hasn’t stepped away fully from the spotlight in the last decade, she’s not at the level of fame she once was. She’s released a few albums in her career, though 2008’s Do You Know was considered a commercial flop.

Jessica hasn’t put out new music in over a decade, with her last album Happy Christmas releasing in 2010. Since then, she’s been focused on her fashion line and raising her family, though she seemingly now feels it’s time to get back in the industry.

However, the same source warns that Jessica isn’t familiar with what the music industry looks like nowadays, so there’s concern any new music could falter like her last couple of albums.

“The music industry has changed completely since Jess was a big star,” they explained. “The days when she could sell 3 million albums are long behind her. Really, she’s a nostalgia act at this point.”

There’s Concern Jessica Could Have A Relapse

The source continued to explain that, given Jessica’s allegedly past issue with substances and alcohol, those close to her fear a relapse if her new projects aren’t received well by fans.

“If this comeback doesn’t work out, there are real fears she’s going to fall into depression and go back to her destructive habits,” they explained. “[Her husband] Eric and her folks are trying to get Jess to manage her expectations — and are monitoring her closely.”

It’s not only rumors – Jessica has been open about her past demons, giving credence to the source’s concerns. In her 2020 memoir Open Book, Simpson admitted to struggling with alcohol and pill addiction, revealing that she turned to substances to cope with past trauma and stress.
